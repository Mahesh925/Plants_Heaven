Plant Disease Information: Corn (maize) - Common Rust

Disease Scientific Name:

Cercospora zeae-maydis

Disease Name:

Corn (maize) - Common Rust

Disease Description:

Common Rust is a fungal disease that affects corn (maize) plants. It is a common foliar disease that can reduce yield and overall crop quality if not managed. Understanding the characteristics, symptoms, transmission, prevention, and management of Common Rust is crucial for corn growers.

Disease Symptoms:

  • Common Rust exhibits the following symptoms:
  • Small Pustules: The most characteristic symptom is the development of small, round to elongated pustules on the leaves, typically on the upper surface. These pustules are rusty brown to reddish-brown in color.
  • Leaf Discoloration: Infected leaves may show yellow or chlorotic streaking between the pustules.
  • Reduced Photosynthesis: Severe infections can reduce photosynthesis, leading to yield loss.

Disease Transmission:

Common Rust is primarily spread through airborne spores. Common transmission routes include: Wind Dissemination: Wind carries fungal spores from infected corn plants to healthy ones.

Disease Prevention:

  • Preventing Common Rust includes the following strategies:
  • Resistant Varieties: Plant corn varieties that are resistant or less susceptible to Common Rust.
  • Crop Rotation: Rotate corn with non-host crops to reduce the buildup of inoculum in the soil.
  • Fungicides: In severe cases, fungicides may be applied preventively.

Disease Management:

  • Effective management of Common Rust involves:
  • Fungicide Applications: In commercial corn production, fungicides are sometimes used to manage the disease. Application timing is critical for effectiveness.
  • Crop Monitoring: Regularly scout corn fields for symptoms and initiate control measures when necessary.
  • Cultural Practices: Implement good agricultural practices, including proper spacing, irrigation management, and residue management, to reduce disease pressure.

By following these guidelines for prevention and management, you can reduce the impact of Corn (maize) - Common Rust on your plants and ensure a healthier harvest.
